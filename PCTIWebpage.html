The journey of P C Training Institute Limited (PCTIL) (formerly P C Training Institute) started in 1990 with the mission of empowering the common man with computer literacy at a very affordable cost at the very start of computer era in India.

Since then, PCTIL has grown into a conglomerate of various business verticals viz Education and Skill Development Training, Technology and Web Solutions, Consultancy, Psychometric Assessments, Healthcare and Travel & Tourism.
